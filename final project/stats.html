PokeSchool

Pokemon share the same 6 stat types

HP The amount of health points that the pokemon has
Attack The amount of damage that the pokemon does with physical attacks
Defense The amount of resistence that the pokemon has against physical attacks
Speed Determines what turn the pokemon moves
Special Defense The amount of resistance that the pokemon has aginst special attacks
Special Attack The amount of damage that the pokemon does with special attacks
