PokeSchool

Welcome to the PokeSchool Website. This website is designed to help people new to the competetive side of pokemon gain knowledge of the competetive side. There are many different aspects that will be covered in this page.

Competetive pokemon is the aspect of pokemon where head to head battles with other players to gain ranks. There are many aspects of competetive pokemon that can seem daunting to new players, this website is going to try to help explain the many different aspects of the competetive scene.
